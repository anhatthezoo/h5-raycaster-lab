Test your level with the rendering view

The ✜ button will define the starting point location (where the camera is placed after the level is loaded).
The ⚙ button will start the 3D rendering where you can move a camera inside your newly created level.
As written at the bottom of the rendering window, use the arrow keys to move the camera, and SPACE to open doors.

Press STOP to exit the rendering view.
